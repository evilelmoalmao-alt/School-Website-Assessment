Love dungeons and dragons but no dungeon master? Love the social aspect of board games or are you looking for some competition? We can offer all of this and more!
Games
The club plays a variety of games such as Dungeons and Dragons, Magic the Gathering, and Wingspan!

As well as social gaming, we also have tournements, both within our club and with other clubs in our region
Schedules
We play D&D on friday evenings, Magic the Gathering of Thursday evenings, and have an open night on Tuesday evenings where groups can play the game of their choice
